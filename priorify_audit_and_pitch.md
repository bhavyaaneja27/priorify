# Priorify: End-to-End Audit & Hackathon Guide

This document contains a complete technical and functional audit of the Priorify platform, followed by resources for your hackathon pitch, unique selling points (USPs), and a 5-minute judge walkthrough.

---

## Part 1: Detailed Codebase Audit

### 1. Authentication (`/login`, `/signup`)
* **Purpose**: Secure user onboarding and identity management.
* **How it works**: Uses Supabase GoTrue auth with email/password, augmented with a "Demo Mode" that bypasses backend auth and uses `localStorage` entirely.
* **User benefit**: Frictionless onboarding for judges (via Demo mode) while retaining enterprise-grade security for real users.
* **AI Integration**: None.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Click "Try Demo" on the login screen to instantly enter the app without creating an account.

### 2. Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
* **Purpose**: The central command center for productivity.
* **How it works**: Aggregates data from 8 different hooks (`useTasks`, `useCalendarEvents`, `useMood`, `useTimetable`, etc.) to render a daily greeting, XP/Streak stats, a dynamic Productivity Score, task completion rings, and an overview of the AI Smart Schedule.
* **User benefit**: Provides a single pane of glass to understand exactly what needs to be done today and how productive you've been.
* **AI Integration**: Displays AI Engine schedules (Local Fallback or Cached Gemini).
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Show the dynamic greeting, point out the XP/Streak counters, and show the "Today's Plan" timeline generated by the engine.

### 3. Tasks & Reminders (`/tasks`)
* **Purpose**: Robust task management with local browser push notifications.
* **How it works**: Users create tasks with priorities, due dates, and specific "due times". The `ReminderEngine` runs in the background, checking `localStorage` every 30 seconds and firing native browser notifications (e.g., "15 min before") when a task is due.
* **User benefit**: Never miss a deadline. Works offline and doesn't require backend Cron jobs.
* **AI Integration**: None directly on this page.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Create a task due 1 minute from now, check "At deadline", and click "Test Notification" to prove to judges that native push notifications work instantly.

### 4. Calendar (`/calendar`)
* **Purpose**: Time-blocking and event management.
* **How it works**: Renders Day, Week, and Month views. It uses a custom `isEventPast` local-timezone parser to hide expired events, keeping the view clean. 
* **User benefit**: Visually block out the day. Automatically hides past clutter.
* **AI Integration**: None.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Toggle "Show Past Events" to demonstrate how the calendar intelligently hides/reveals history. Show the elegant frosted-glass empty state if no events exist.

### 5. AI Priority Engine (`/ai-engine`)
* **Purpose**: AI-driven task sorting, risk analysis, and schedule generation.
* **How it works**: Evaluates tasks locally using `scoreTask` (factoring in due dates, priorities, calendar overlaps). Generates a "Smart Schedule" by slotting tasks into free calendar gaps. Uses `buildLocalDailyPlan` by default to save quota, falling back to Gemini only on manual refresh.
* **User benefit**: Eliminates decision fatigue by telling the user exactly what to do and when to do it.
* **AI Integration**: **Local Analysis (Default) / Real Gemini (Manual)**.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Click the magic wand icon to instantly sort tasks by AI Score. Highlight the red/yellow/green Risk Badges. Show the "Smart Schedule" that builds a timeline around existing calendar events.

### 6. Focus Sessions (`/focus`)
* **Purpose**: Pomodoro timer for deep work.
* **How it works**: A customizable timer that integrates with the user's XP and Level system. Completing sessions writes to `pomodoro_history` (Supabase or local).
* **User benefit**: Enforces focused work blocks, gamified with XP rewards.
* **AI Integration**: None.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Start a session, explain how it awards XP, and show how the UI cleanly updates.

### 7. Productivity Check (`/productivity-check`)
* **Purpose**: Energy and mood tracking.
* **How it works**: Simple UI to select an energy level (emoji). Saves data to `productivity_checks` to map energy vs. focus over time.
* **User benefit**: Helps users understand their peak performance hours.
* **AI Integration**: None.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Click an emoji to log energy, then show how it reflects on the Dashboard stress/focus charts.

### 8. Insights (`/insights`)
* **Purpose**: Gamification and long-term analytics.
* **How it works**: Calculates a proprietary Productivity Score based on streaks, total focus hours, and completed plans. Unlocks achievements dynamically.
* **User benefit**: Keeps users motivated through gamified progression (XP, Levels, Badges).
* **AI Integration**: None.
* **Current Status**: **Fully Functional**.
* **Demo Flow**: Scroll through unlocked badges (e.g., "Deep Focus", "Productivity Streak") and point out the XP total.

### 9. Action Planner (`/action-planner` / `AIPlanner.tsx`)
* **Purpose**: Breaking down massive goals into step-by-step plans.
* **Status**: **Placeholder / Coming Soon**.
* **How it works**: Currently renders a beautiful "Planned for Future Release" frosted-glass card with pulsing effects.
* **Demo Flow**: Briefly flash this page to show the product's future roadmap and vision, proving you have a clear plan for expansion.

## Part 2: Hackathon Pitch Guide

**Elevator Pitch (30 seconds):**
"Hi everyone, we built **Priorify**. It's not just another to-do list—it's an intelligent productivity operating system designed for the modern professional. Most tools give you a blank page and expect you to organize your life. Priorify uses an AI Priority Engine that evaluates your deadlines, priorities, and existing calendar conflicts to literally tell you what to do next. We've combined gamification, local push notifications, and smart scheduling into one stunning, distraction-free interface."

**The Problem:**
"Decision fatigue. People spend more time *managing* their tasks than actually *doing* them. We stare at long lists of overdue items, get overwhelmed, and procrastinate."

**The Solution:**
"Priorify’s local analysis engine scores every task dynamically. It spots deadline risks before you miss them. It takes your calendar events and automatically slots your highest-priority tasks into your free time, building a daily plan for you."

**The Tech Stack:**
"We built this using React, TailwindCSS, and Supabase. What’s truly innovative is our hybrid AI architecture. To ensure lightning-fast performance and zero API costs for heavy usage, our primary sorting and risk analysis runs 100% locally in the browser. We only trigger the Gemini API on-demand for complex schedule generation, keeping our app endlessly scalable on the free tier."

## Part 3: Unique Selling Points (USPs) & Innovation

1. **Hybrid AI Architecture (Zero-Latency / Zero-Cost Scaling)**
   * **Innovation**: Instead of hitting the Gemini API on every page load, Priorify uses a highly optimized local heuristic algorithm (`scoreTask`) to rank tasks and calculate risks. Gemini is reserved only for complex, manual generations. This ensures a snappy UX and virtually eliminates API costs.
2. **Calendar-Aware Task Slotting**
   * **USP**: Priorify doesn't just list tasks. It reads your calendar, finds the gaps between your meetings, and automatically slots tasks into those free blocks.
3. **True Browser-Native Reminders**
   * **Innovation**: We built a `ReminderEngine` that hooks into the native browser Notification API. It persists via local storage and fires 15-minute warnings without requiring a complex backend Cron job infrastructure.
4. **Gamified Productivity (XP & Levels)**
   * **USP**: Every completed task and focus session awards XP, unlocking achievements and visual badges on the Insights dashboard, keeping users addicted to productivity.
5. **Frictionless Demo Mode**
   * **Innovation**: A fully functional local-storage fallback layer. Judges don't have to create an account or verify emails; they click "Try Demo" and the entire app (including AI features and data persistence) works perfectly in the browser.
